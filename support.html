Common questions

Frequently asked

How do I onboard teachers and students in bulk?+

From the Admin dashboard you can import teachers, students and parents in bulk, then assign roles, classes and sections. Our team helps with the first import during setup.

Does ReViSense.AI work offline?+

Core school-management functionality includes offline support, syncing automatically when the connection returns. AI features require an internet connection.

How is student screen time protected?+

A built-in 40-minute eye-protection app lock pauses usage to protect eyesight, and teachers control exactly what the AI can teach.

Can parents track the school bus?+

Yes, with the Premium package. Parents get real-time GPS bus tracking, route history and live notifications.

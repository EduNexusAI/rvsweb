Refund Policy

Our policy on trials, subscription refunds and hardware returns for ReViSense.AI.

On this page
1. Free trial first 2. Software subscriptions 3. Hardware returns 4. How to request a refund 5. Changes to this policy
Last updated: 30 May 2026 · ReViSense.AI by TrioGenX Groups LLP

1. Free trial first

Every school gets a 15-day full-feature trial with complete support before paying. We strongly encourage using the trial to confirm fit, so you only subscribe when you’re confident.

2. Software subscriptions

  • Subscription fees are billed per your order and are generally non-refundable once a billing period has begun.
  • If you experience a significant service issue we cannot resolve, contact us within 7 days of the charge and we will review eligibility for a pro-rated credit or refund in good faith.
  • Introductory offers (e.g. first-30-institutes “pay for 10 months”) are promotional and non-transferable.

3. Hardware returns

  • Hardware (3D Hologram Fan, AI Digital Board) may be returned only if dead-on-arrival or defective, reported within 7 days of delivery.
  • Returned hardware must be in original condition and packaging. Installation, customisation and shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • Manufacturer warranty terms apply as specified in your purchase order.

4. How to request a refund

Email [email protected] with your institution name, invoice number and reason. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–14 business days.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The version in effect at the time of your purchase governs that transaction.

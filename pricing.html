Prices shown are exclusive of GST (marked +GST). Your final invoice will include applicable taxes with full transparency — no hidden charges.

Every school gets a 15-day full-feature trial with complete onboarding support. The first 30 institutes also pay only for 10 months of the year — the first 2 months are free.

Yes. You can upgrade from Advance to Premium, or move to a fully customised Elite plan at any time. Your data and configuration carry over automatically.

Privacy Policy

How ReViSense.AI collects, uses and protects personal and student data across our platform and services.

On this page
1. Overview 2. Information we collect 3. How we use information 4. AI & student safety 5. Sharing & disclosure 6. Data retention 7. Your rights 8. Security
Last updated: 30 May 2026 · ReViSense.AI by TrioGenX Groups LLP

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how TrioGenX Groups LLP (“ReViSense.AI”) handles personal information when institutions, staff, parents and students use our Services. We are committed to protecting student data and using it only for legitimate educational purposes.

2. Information we collect

  • Institution & contact data: school name, address, contact person, phone and email.
  • User data: names, roles, classes, attendance, results and usage activity.
  • Student learning data: AI study interactions, progress and performance metrics.
  • Technical data: device, log and diagnostic information needed to run the Services.

3. How we use information

  • Provide attendance, analytics, AI learning and management features.
  • Generate reports and insights for authorised roles.
  • Improve, secure and support the Services.
  • Communicate about your account, demos and support requests.

4. AI & student safety

Our study AI is designed to be hallucination-free and operates within teacher-controlled boundaries. AI interactions are used to support learning and are not sold to third parties. Eye-protection app locks help safeguard student wellbeing.

5. Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We share information only with trusted processors who help us deliver the Services, and where required by law. Institutions remain the data controllers for their student records.

6. Data retention

We retain data for as long as your institution uses the Services or as required by law. On termination, data can be exported and is then deleted or anonymised within a reasonable period.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, individuals may request access, correction or deletion of their personal data. Requests from students or parents should be routed through the institution; we will assist as the processor.

8. Security

We apply institution-grade safeguards including encryption in transit, access controls and regular backups. See our Data Security page for details.

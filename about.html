About ReViSense.AI

We’re building the AI operating system for education

ReViSense.AI unifies attendance, analytics, AI learning, mentor intelligence and institutional automation into one ecosystem — so every school can run smarter and every learner can go further.

Our story

From classrooms, for classrooms

Indian schools were juggling a dozen disconnected tools — one for attendance, another for results, a third for messaging — and none of them spoke the language of the classroom. TrioGenX Groups LLP set out to change that.

We built ReViSense.AI as a single, intelligent platform: a hallucination-free study AI trained for Indian classrooms, immersive 3D holograms that bring concepts to life, AI digital boards, and analytics that help administrators decide faster.

Today, ReViSense.AI helps schools across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra consolidate learning, power active revision, and give every role — student, teacher, mentor, parent and admin — the intelligence they need.

Data Security

How ReViSense.AI protects institution and student data with enterprise-grade security.

On this page
1. Our commitment 2. Infrastructure & encryption 3. Access controls 4. Student safety 5. Compliance 6. Reporting a concern
Last updated: 30 May 2026 · ReViSense.AI by TrioGenX Groups LLP

1. Our commitment

Schools trust us with sensitive student information. We treat that responsibility seriously and build security into every layer of the ReViSense.AI platform.

2. Infrastructure & encryption

  • Data encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS.
  • Hosted on reputable cloud infrastructure with redundancy and regular backups.
  • Targeted high availability with monitoring and incident response.

3. Access controls

  • Strict role-based access for students, teachers, mentors, parents and admins.
  • Least-privilege principles and audit trails for sensitive actions.
  • Teacher-controlled boundaries on AI features.

4. Student safety

Built-in protections include a 40-minute eye-protection app lock and supervised, hallucination-free AI so students cannot misuse the learning tools.

5. Compliance

We build to meet institutional data-protection and privacy requirements applicable in India, and align our practices with our Privacy Policy.

6. Reporting a concern
